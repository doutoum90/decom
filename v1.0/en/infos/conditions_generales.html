3. PRESENTATION OF DIGIPOSTE

Within the framework of the Digiposte Service, the Subscriber enjoys various features. Some of these features are accessible only through the Application.

  • Archiving space : enables Content to be archived and/or filed. The initial storage capacity is stated on the Digiposte Website. Documents for which the legal storage period or the storage period contractually fixed with the Issuer has expired shall be deducted from the archiving space.
  • The sharing space : enables Subscribers to share their Content temporarily with third parties. Sharing is the full responsibility of the Subscriber. Shared documents will be accessible to third parties through a common link communicated to the Subscriber and for the duration defined by the latter. The Subscriber shall be solely responsible for the use to which this link is put. Subscribers are hereby informed that publication of this link on open or unsecured spaces can make it possible for any person clicking on this link to access the shared documents.
    A code can be set to guarantee access only to the person who has it.
  • Documents collector : enables Subscribers to authorise La Poste to log into third-party websites from which they usually access their documents in order to retrieve Content, in their name and on their behalf. Subscribers will have to enter their login information (username/password) for each website concerned. Subscribers remain solely responsible for the use of their login information. They also confirm that they have all the rights required to enable La Poste to collect their documents.
    La Poste shall decide by operation of law on the possible removal of a collector of documents from the list of available third party entities and to cease to offer the feature of synchronisation and updating of documents issued by said third party entity. The Content recovered prior to the removal of the collector shall be stored in the archiving space.
  • Procedure creation : Subscribers can carry out personal or administrative procedures by grouping all or some of the information and documents required. The documents contained in these procedures can be sent to a third party addressee solely by the Subscriber. Some specific procedures can be offered by third parties depending on the specific information the Subscriber shared with the third party. La Poste must use its best efforts to ensure conformity between the information and documents linked to the procedures and may under no circumstances be held responsible for non-conformity or unsuitability of this information or documents.
  • Electronic stamp visible : allows a Subscriber to print a document by adding an encrypted barcode created in line with the standard set by the Agence Nationale des Titres Sécurisés (ANTS). This barcode contains the key information of the Document, the date of issue of the Document or 2D Code and the electronic signature of the hash of these data. It can be read by compatible mobile applications and via a website (Digicheck.fr). This code allows the User to verify that the information provided on the Document and verified by the User is consistent with that provided by any Issuer to produce the 2D Code. It makes it possible to secure the Documents used as supporting documents, for example in the context of administrative procedures.
  • Merging Digiposte Accounts : allows the Subscriber to merge two Digiposte Accounts. Digiposte Accounts can be merged from the Digiposte Account that the Subscriber wishes to keep and allows the Subscriber to retrieve Content from another Digiposte Account. Memberships to Issuers and/or Collectors of documents are also recovered.
  • *Health Data : this service enables, with the prior agreement of the Subscriber and under their control, the hosting and management of their Health Data.
    Digiposte complies with the regulatory framework applicable to health data hosting (HDS).
  • Subscriber Document Flow Subscription : allows the Subscriber to receive the original digital Documents directly from a Sender in their Digiposte Account. There are various ways to subscribe. The user can subscribe to a Flow directly through the Digiposte Service by filling out a form to subscribe to the Document of the Sender concerned. Alternatively, the user can subscribe directly to the Sender under the conditions stipulated by the Sender. Depending on the nature of the documents and / or the contractual relationship between the Sender and the Subscriber, subscription may be achieved by signing a Digitisation Agreement. The received documents will be stored during the specific retention period stated in the contract by each Sender. Original digital documents are not counted in the free offer storage space, and can thus be stored throughout the statutory retention period set by the Sender and Digiposte, without the Subscriber having to subscribe for additional storage space.
  • Notification Notification : The Application may send Subscribers information or propose actions and procedures, based on their usage or the documents they have stored in Digiposte. Subscribers can manage their notifications in the Application’s settings.
